Tiramisu

Description

Transport yourself to Italy with a slice of Tiramisu, a decadent and luscious dessert that delights the senses. Layers of delicate ladyfinger cookies are delicately soaked in coffee and liqueur, if desired, creating a moist and flavorful base. These coffee-infused cookies are then layered with a velvety mixture of mascarpone cheese, sugar, and vanilla extract, resulting in a creamy and indulgent filling. To finish, a dusting of cocoa powder adds a touch of bitterness and elegance. Refrigerated to perfection, each bite of Tiramisu offers a harmonious blend of textures and flavors, making it the perfect finale to any meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups brewed strong coffee, cooled
  • 2 tablespoons rum or coffee liqueur (optional)
  • 24 ladyfinger cookies
  • 2 cups mascarpone cheese
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder

Instructions:

  1. In a shallow dish, combine the brewed coffee and rum or coffee liqueur (if using).
  2. Dip each ladyfinger cookie into the coffee mixture for a few seconds, allowing it to soak up the liquid without becoming too soggy.
  3. Arrange half of the soaked ladyfingers in a single layer in the bottom of a serving dish.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine the mascarpone cheese, sugar, and vanilla extract. Beat until smooth and creamy.
  5. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the soaked ladyfingers in the dish.
  6. Repeat the layers with the remaining soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone mixture.
  7. Sift the cocoa powder over the top layer of the tiramisu.
  8. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight, to allow the flavors to meld together.
  9. Serve chilled and enjoy this classic Italian dessert!
