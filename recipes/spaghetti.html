Spaghetti

Description

Indulge in the rich and savory flavors of Spaghetti Bolognese, a classic Italian pasta dish. This recipe combines al dente spaghetti noodles with a hearty meat sauce made from ground beef, onions, garlic, carrots, and celery. The sauce is simmered to perfection with crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and a blend of aromatic herbs. The result is a mouthwatering Bolognese sauce that beautifully coats each strand of pasta. Topped with a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese, this comforting and satisfying dish is a favorite for pasta lovers.

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces spaghetti
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, finely diced
  • 1 stalk celery, finely diced
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion, garlic, carrot, and celery. Saut√© until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Add the ground beef to the skillet. Cook, breaking it up with a spoon, until browned and cooked through.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and black pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Serve the spaghetti with the Bolognese sauce on top. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese. Enjoy!
