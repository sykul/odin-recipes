Lasagne

Description

The lasagne recipe is a classic Italian dish that consists of layers of delicious flavors and textures. This hearty and comforting dish starts with a rich meat sauce made with ground beef, onions, garlic, crushed tomatoes, and aromatic herbs like basil and oregano. The sauce simmers to perfection, allowing the flavors to meld together. Meanwhile, lasagne noodles are cooked until al dente and drained. The assembly begins with a layer of meat sauce at the bottom of a baking dish, followed by a layer of tender lasagne noodles. The noodles are then topped with a creamy ricotta cheese mixture and a generous sprinkling of mozzarella cheese. This layering process is repeated, creating a delectable tower of flavors. Finally, the lasagne is baked in the oven until golden and bubbling, resulting in a mouthwatering dish that is sure to impress. Allow the lasagne to cool slightly before serving, and savor each bite of this comforting and satisfying Italian classic.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 12 lasagne noodles, cooked and drained
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until the meat is browned and the onion is translucent.
  2. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, and black pepper to the skillet. Stir well to combine. Simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  4. In a greased 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a layer of the meat sauce on the bottom. Arrange 4 lasagne noodles on top of the sauce.
  5. Spoon half of the ricotta cheese evenly over the noodles. Sprinkle 1 cup of mozzarella cheese on top. Repeat the layers with the remaining sauce, noodles, ricotta cheese, and mozzarella cheese.
  6. Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese evenly over the top layer.
  7. Cover the baking dish with aluminium foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  8. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the lasagne is hot and bubbling.
  9. Allow the lasagne to cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
